About Diseases

Brown Blight

Brown blight causes small, light-green spots on young leaves which later become blister-like lesions. It affects leaf development severely.

Black Rot

Black rot appears as irregular black patches on the leaf, usually starting from the edge. It is caused by fungi and spreads in humid conditions.

Leaf Rust

Leaf rust creates small rust-colored pustules on the underside of leaves, reducing photosynthesis and weakening the plant.

Mosquito Bug

Mosquito bug causes dark patches covered with moss-like fungal growth. It thrives in wet, shaded environments.

Red Spider Mite

This pest sucks sap from the leaves, leading to speckled yellowing. Infestation causes leaf drop and reduced yield.

White Scab

White scab appears as dry, scabby white spots on leaves. Severe cases can deform leaves and reduce plant growth.
